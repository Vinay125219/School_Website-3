Assessment & Progression

Our assessment system is designed to evaluate students' understanding, skills, and growth. We follow a continuous and comprehensive evaluation approach that includes:

  • Formative assessments through classwork, homework, projects, and activities
  • Periodic tests and quizzes to gauge subject understanding
  • Term-end examinations with a focus on conceptual clarity
  • Portfolio assessment to track progress over time
  • Comprehensive feedback system for students and parents

Students receive detailed progress reports that highlight their strengths and areas for improvement, providing a roadmap for their academic growth.
