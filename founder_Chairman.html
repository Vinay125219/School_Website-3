Founder Chairman

Dr. Rajendra Kumar

Founder & Chairman

"Education is not preparation for life; education is life itself."

Dr. Rajendra Kumar founded Spring Wood School in 1985 with a vision to provide quality education that focuses on holistic development of students. With over 40 years of experience in the education sector, he has transformed the landscape of education through his innovative approaches and unwavering commitment.

Under his visionary leadership, Spring Wood School has grown from a small institution to one of the most prestigious educational establishments in the region, known for academic excellence and character building.

Dr. Kumar holds a Ph.D. in Educational Leadership from Oxford University and has authored several books on educational reforms and pedagogical innovations. His philosophy emphasizes on learning by doing and encourages students to develop critical thinking skills rather than rote learning.

His contributions to the field of education have been recognized nationally and internationally, earning him numerous accolades including the National Award for Excellence in Education Leadership in 2010.
